## What Can Be Reused

Useful structure:

- Home / Portfolio / Project / Contact page split.
- Hero plus proof-projects plus CTA shape.
- Project detail page with problem, body, benefits, stats, gallery.
- Contact page structure.
- Sitemap and wireframe as a starting IA.

Useful copy direction:

- "Building AI products that matter"
- "A selection of AI and product work built to solve real problems"
- "Ready to build something real"

## What Must Not Be Used Directly

Invented or unsafe:

- Fake project names: Sentiment analysis tool, Recommendation engine, Chat interface.
- Fake metrics: 94% accuracy, 23% engagement increase.
- Fake testimonials and fake names.
- Fake institutions and companies: Stanford, Acme, TechCorp.
- Fake candidate names: Alex Morgan, Jordan Lee, Casey Williams, Riley Park.
- Generic placeholder content: "Medium length section heading goes here", lorem ipsum.
- Newsletter and blog sections unless the candidate really has writing.
- Login / Apply navigation unless the site has those flows.

## Product Takeaway

Relume is useful for information architecture and wireframe suggestions, but it should not be trusted for factual resume content.

For Offer-Compass, generated sections must be constrained by:

- Parsed resume facts.
- User-approved public proof points.
- Privacy redaction.
- No invented metrics, people, schools, companies, testimonials, awards, or links.
- A final hallucination and privacy review before publishing.
